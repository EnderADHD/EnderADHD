Jumpstyle

Jump style is a very cool type of dance that originated in Belgium and the Netherlands. It is characterized by fast movements, high jumps, and rhythmic footwork. Dancers often wear oversize clothes such as hoodies and pants, and the dance is typically performed to electronic music with a fast tempo. Jump style has gained popularity in various dance communities around the world, and it is often associated with the year 2007.
